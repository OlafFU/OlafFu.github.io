I am a Master's student in Building Performance Diagnostics at **Carnegie Mellon University** (2025–2027), with a BEng in Architectural Environment Engineering from the **University of Nottingham** (First Class Honours).

My work focuses on building energy simulation, HVAC system design, fault detection, and low-carbon retrofit strategies. I have project experience across the US, UK, and China.

## Research

My thesis research investigates passive cooling solutions to enhance solar panel efficiency. A related paper has been published in the *International Journal of Thermophysics*: [doi.org/10.1007/s10765-025-03649-8](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10765-025-03649-8)

I also contributed to a demand-side HVAC system model for industrial buildings using Modelica, published in *Applied Energy*: [doi.org/10.1016/j.apenergy.2024.124971](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.apenergy.2024.124971)
